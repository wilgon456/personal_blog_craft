# Search Console Checklist

1. Open Google Search Console and add `https://tuchizblog.today/` as a property.
2. Verify ownership with your preferred method.
3. Submit the sitemap URL: `https://tuchizblog.today/sitemap.xml`
4. Check the indexing report after the next deployment.
5. Monitor:
   - Pages indexed
   - Breadcrumb rich results
   - Article rich results
   - Core Web Vitals

Notes:
- Internal search pages are marked `noindex` and are not included in the sitemap.
- The sitemap includes the homepage, about, archive, series, posts, and tag pages.
